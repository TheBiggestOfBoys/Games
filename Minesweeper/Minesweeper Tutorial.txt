The goal of minesweeper is to flag all the mines without accidentally clicking on one.
You can flag suspected mines by pressing "SPACE", turning the '?' into a '!'.
You can dig suspected safe tiles by pressing "ENTER", turning the '?' into the number of surrounding mines, or '*' if you guessed wrong (it was a mine.
Navigate up and down on the grid using "UP ARROW" & "DOWN ARROW".
Navigate left and right on the grid using "LEFT ARROW" & "RIGHT ARROW".
Key:
    ?' is a hidden tile
    !' is a flagged tile
    '*' is a mine
    '2' etc. amount of surrounding mines
